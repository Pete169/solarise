    Questions

    Things people ask us

    How long does installation take?

    Most residential systems are installed and commissioned within 1–2 days after the survey and payment confirmation.

    Will it really replace my generator?

    Yes. A correctly sized hybrid system runs your home day and night, charging batteries from the sun and topping up from grid when needed — no fuel required.

    What if there's no sun for days?

    Hybrid inverters can also charge batteries from the grid, so you stay powered even during extended cloudy periods.

    Do you offer payment plans?

    Yes — we offer flexible plans from 6 to 24 months. Ask during your survey and we'll tailor one to your budget.

    What warranty do I get?

    Up to 10 years on inverters, 5–10 years on lithium batteries, and 25-year performance warranty on panels, depending on package.

    Do you install outside my city?

    We cover major cities nationwide and can arrange installs in surrounding areas. Send your location and we'll confirm.
