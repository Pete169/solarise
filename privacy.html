Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

SolaRise ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect through this website, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We aim to comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023.

What we collect

  • Enquiry details you send us — when you request a consultation, we collect your name, phone number, location, the system you're interested in, anything you write in the message box, and your email address if you choose to add it.
  • WhatsApp messages — if you contact us on WhatsApp, your chat is handled by WhatsApp under WhatsApp's own privacy policy.
  • Usage analytics (only with your consent) — if you accept cookies, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (pages viewed, tools used, device type). This never includes what you type into our forms.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry, arrange your free consultation, and provide quotes.
  • To improve the website based on how visitors use it.
  • We do not sell your personal information, and we don't add you to marketing lists without asking.

Who processes your data

  • Formspree delivers your enquiry to our inbox (Formspree privacy policy).
  • Google Analytics processes anonymous usage statistics, only after you accept cookies (Google privacy policy).

Cookies & your choices

Analytics cookies load only if you tap "Accept" on our cookie banner. Declining changes nothing about how the site works for you. You can change your mind at any time:

Reset my cookie choice — the banner will show again on your next visit.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry details for as long as needed to follow up and serve you as a customer. You can ask us to delete your details at any time.

Your rights

Under the NDPA you can ask us what information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Just message or call us using the details below.
